Company

Scarlett Turner

Creative Director

Scarlett is a graduate of Brighton (2013) and De Montfort University (2015). Her practice focuses on physical theatre and contemporary dance methods. Currently Scarlett is a breakthrough artists at Curve Theatre and is Artistic Director of Fuelled Dance Theatre, supported by Attenborough Arts Center. Read more...

In addition to working with Fuelled Dance Theatre Scarlett is currently working on a solo physical theatre piece dealing with the themes of identity, society's norms, gender and sexuality, called Come As You Are. In September 2017 this was workshopped to international artists as part of a dance residency in Austria.

They have been involved in other various projects. Live artist Priya Mistry as a movement practitioner for her show ‘Mental Health Cabaret.’ Performer, in Lucy Suggate ‘Swarm Sculptures’ at Dance4 Launch and performed four times in D.A.N.C.E and is currently associate choreographer with David Ralfe, On the run theatre in his new play Captain Simon.

Fern Chubb

Dance Artist

Fern has been a performer in Fuelled Dance Theatre for teh past year and has previously trained in Contemporary Dance at The University of Northampton. Fern has also collaborated with A Bang in the Void, Highly Sprung Performance Co. and Vortex Creates as well as presenting her own project collaborating with actor and performance artist Jonathan McClean in their company Scuffed Shoes Theatre.

Harriet Holman

Dance Artist

Harriet has been a member of Fuelled Dance Theatre since June 2017 and she is a recent BA Dance (Hons) graduate of De Montfort University. She is also trained in Ballet by the Royal Academy of Dance. Harriet dances in university-led company Void Dance Company and was a performer for Lucy Suggate's live art installation ‘Swarm Sculptures’.

Nadine Knew

Dance Artist

Nadine has been a performer with Fuelled Dance Theatre since June 2017. A recent graduate in Dance from De Montfort University, Nadine works with movement improvisation and voice. Before Graduating she has already begun creating her own solo work 'She is 12', which was shown at Attenborough Arts Center in July 2017.

Liza Mortimer

Dance Artist

Liza is a new member of Fuelled Dance Theatre, joining the company in June 2017 . She recently graduated from De Montfort University in Dance. In October 2015 Liza was part of a collective that created a video adaptation of the song ‘Corycian Fire’ by Steve Hackett, which included a contemporary twist on Ancient Greek/ Folk dance style for film.

Dani Spooner

Dance Artist

Danni Spooner has performed in Fuelled for the past year and is trained in ballet, jazz and musical theatre. She studied her BA (Hons) in Contemporary Dance at De Montfort University. Danni is also known for her film work 'FAG' with Random Acts and as a dancer in two Goldfrapp music videos.

Matt Cawrey

Photographer

Matt has been Fuelled's house photographer since 2015, but has now also started filmography with Fuelled Dance Theatre. He is a Midlands-based performance and documentary photographer, working with artists and performers across the country on projects including theatre, dance, and performance art. He is also regular contributer to Leicester’s Great Central Magazine. Matt’s ethos is to document performance whilst having a minimal impact, with the aim of capturing the essence of the work.

Samuel Warren

Musician

Samuel Warren has a BA (hons) in Music tech and innovation and is currently undertaking his research masters at DMU. His research is focused on the relationship between acousmatic music and movement practice. Sam has also collaborated with Fuelled once before on our previous work ‘Enpalthy’. Sam is a professional creative composer who produced innovative work that merges with Fuelled Dance Theatre's style of choreography.

Michael Riley

Designer/Developer

Michael is a designer and front-end web developer. He's a junkie for beautiful, functional design and his company, Follow The Animal, works with Fuelled on their branding and web presence. When he's not building the web Michael can usuallly be found playing guitar, studying Software Development, cooking cajun food and arguing with his cats.
