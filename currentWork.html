Current Work

This Is Sense? (2017)

The first research and development period for 'This Is Sense?' started in 2016, when Fuelled was awared a Grant For The Arts by Arts Council England.

'This Is Sense?' explores the weird and wonderul ways in which we as people see ourselves through the social politics of self-identity.

Questioning and sometimes answering, ‘Who I am?’, the work shows the rollercoaster that people go through to discover what it is to be them. Through their own personal stories and how they cope with the fake personality they create to get through difficult situations. The true storytellings of their lives that have made them understand a part of themselves.
